How it works

BICEP spawns thousands of particles that explore paths in parallel. Each particle follows a Brownian bridge dynamics with a gentle pull toward the goal: 

x_{k+1} = x_k + (goal - x_k)/(N - k) + σ·Z_k

• Parallel exploration: All particles move simultaneously
• Goal attraction: Stronger pull as time runs out
• Stochastic noise: σ controls exploration vs exploitation
• Collision handling: Particles die on obstacle contact
